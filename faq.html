Frequently Asked Questions

The Hello Kitty Cafe is a themed cafe based on Sanrio's iconic character, Hello Kitty, located in the Irvine Spectrum Center in Irvine, CA. We have a daily cafe, an afternoon tea service, and a cocktail room avaliable to guests.

In order to support local independent farms in the Southern California area, we have a small fixed menu avaliable to guests year round. However, during certain times of the year, we will have a seasonal menu in addition to our regular menu, which can range from a meyer lemon themed menu in the summmertime, a blueberry themed menu in the spring, and a pumpkin and apple themed menu in the chilly winter. The menus currently displayed on our website are not representative of our avaliable menu in store. If you would like to know the complete menu as of this time, you can call directly.

Reservations are highly recommended for guaranteed seating for our cocktail service and afternoon tea sessions. We cannot anticipate how many guests will walk in and we will prioritize those who have reserved over walk in guests. You can reserve a spot for either services on our home page. After you fill out the form, a host contact you for additional details.
