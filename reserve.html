Reservation Information

Reservations are exclusively for our Afternoon Tea & Cocktail Service, located in the Bow Room. Walk-in's are welcomed but only based on avaliability and are not guaranteed. Our daily cafe does not require a reservation.Our Afternoon Tea sessions are $60 per persion, with a prix fixe menu that consists of sweet and savory items and a pot of tea of your choice.Our Cocktail Service is for 21+ guests only; minors will not be allowed into the Bow Room during this time and will be turned away if guests do not have a valid form of ID indicating they are of age.We respectfully ask for confirmation and party changes to be made at least 24 hours prior to the reservation time. Without prior confirmation, tables are subject to be forfeited after a 15 minute grace period.
